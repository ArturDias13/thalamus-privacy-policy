Thalamus Privacy Policy

Thalamus is a corporate application (MES – Manufacturing Execution System) intended for use by authorized users of specific companies. This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, and protected.

Personal Data Collection

To enable authentication and proper operation of the application, Thalamus collects and stores the following personal data:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Authentication information (access tokens)

These data are provided by the user in the context of corporate use and are strictly necessary for account management and access control.

Use of Information

The collected information is used exclusively for:

  • User authentication and authorization
  • Account identification within the system
  • Operational activity logging and traceability
  • Security and proper functioning of the application

Visibility of Operational Records

In corporate environments, certain operational records (such as machine inspection data and the identification of the responsible user) may be visible to other authorized users within the same organization. This visibility is limited to operational and internal control purposes.

Camera and Image Access

Thalamus may request access to the device camera and photo gallery to support features related to the application’s operational and functional use.

Such access may allow users to capture new images using the camera or select existing images from their device gallery in order to document, validate, or support activities performed within the system.

All images are provided voluntarily by the user and are used exclusively for internal, operational, traceability, and quality-related purposes associated with the application’s functionality.

Images may be stored and linked to system records and can be accessed only by authorized users within the same organization. Thalamus does not access the camera or gallery without user interaction, and does not use images for tracking, advertising, or unrelated purposes.

Third-Party Services

Thalamus uses Firebase Crashlytics to collect crash reports and diagnostic information. This data is technical in nature and is used exclusively to improve application stability and performance. The information collected by this service is not used for tracking or advertising purposes.

Data Sharing

We do not share personal user data with third parties, except when required by law or when necessary to provide essential services related to the application’s operation.

Data Storage and Security

User data is stored on proprietary backend infrastructure located in Brazil. Appropriate technical and organizational measures are applied to protect personal data against unauthorized access, loss, or misuse.

User Rights

Users may request access to, correction of, or deletion of their personal data when applicable. Requests can be made through the contact information provided below.

Children’s Privacy

Thalamus is not intended for use by children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. Users are encouraged to review it regularly.
